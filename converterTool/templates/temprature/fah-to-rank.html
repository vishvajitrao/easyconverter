How to convert Fahrenheit To Rankine

0 degree Fahrenheit is equal to the 459.67 degrees Rankine.

0°F = 459.67 °R

The temperature T in degrees Kelvin (K) is equal to the temperature T in degrees Fahrenheit plus 459.67:

T(K) = (T(°F) + 459.67 )

Example

Convert 20 degrees Fahrenheit to Rankine degrees.

T(K) = (20(°F) + 459.67 )= 266.483K

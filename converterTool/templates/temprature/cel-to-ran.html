Formula

How to convert Celsius To Rankine

0 degree Celsius is equal to the 273.15 kelvin.

0 °C = 273.15K

The temperature T in degrees Rankine (°R) is equal to the temperature T in degrees Celsius plus 273.15,times 9/5:

T(°R) = (T(°C) + 273.15K ) x 9/5

Example

Convert 10 degrees Celsius to Rankine degrees.

T(°R) = (10(°C) + 273.15K ) x 9/5 = 509.67 (°R)

Formula

How to convert Kelvin To Fahrenheit

0 degree Kelvin is equal to the -459.67 degrees Fahrenheit.

0 K = -459.67 °F

The temperature T in degrees Fahrenheit (°F) is equal to the temperature T in degrees Kelvin minus 273.15,Plus 32, times 9/5:

T(°C) = (T(K) -273.15 ) x 9/5 + 32

Example

Convert 20 degrees Kelvin to Fahrenheit degrees.

T(°C) = (20(K) -273.15 ) x 9/5 + 32 = -253.15°C

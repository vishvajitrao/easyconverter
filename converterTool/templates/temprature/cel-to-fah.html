How to convert Celsius To Fahrenheit

0 degree Celsius is equal to the 32 degree Fahrenheit.

0 °C = 32 °F

The temperature T in degrees Fahrenheit (°F) is equal to the temperature T in degrees Celsius (°C) times 9/5 plus 32:

T(°F) = T(°C) × 9/5 + 32

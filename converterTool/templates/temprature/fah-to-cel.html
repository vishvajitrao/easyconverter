How to convert Fahrenheit To Celsius

0 degree Fahrenheit is equal to the -17.77778 degrees Celsius.

0°F = -17.77778 °C

The temperature T in degrees Celsius (°C) is equal to the temperature T in degrees Fahrenheit minus 32,times 5/9:

T(°R) = (T(°C) + 273.15K ) x 5/9

Example

Convert 10 degrees Fahrenheit to Celsius degrees.

T(°C) = (10(°F) - 32 ) x 5/9 = -12.2222 (°C)

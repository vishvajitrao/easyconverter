How to convert Rankine To Kelvin

The temperature T in degrees Kelvin (K) is equal to the temperature T in degrees Rankine,times 5/9:

T(K) = ( T(°R) x 5/9 )

Example

Convert 200 degrees Rankine to kelvin degrees.

T(K) = 200°R x 5/9 = 111.11 K

Formula

How to convert Rankine To Celsius

The temperature T in degrees Celsius (°C) is equal to the temperature T in degrees Rankine minus 491.67, times 5/9:

T(°C) = ( T(°R) - 491.67 ) x 5/9

Example

Convert 200 degrees Rankine to Celsius degrees.

T(°C) = ( 200(°R) - 491.67 ) x 5/9 = -162.04 °C
